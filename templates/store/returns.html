Return Policy

30-Day Return Policy

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not happy with your order for any reason, you can return it within 30 days of delivery for a full refund.

Return Eligibility

To be eligible for a return, your item must be:

  • Unworn, unwashed, and unaltered
  • In its original packaging with all tags attached
  • Free from any scents or odors
  • Purchased directly from StyleHub

Note: Sale items and intimates are final sale and cannot be returned unless defective.

How to Return an Item

  1. Log in to your account
    Visit your Order History and find the order containing the items you wish to return.
  2. Initiate your return
    Click "Return Items" and select the products you want to return along with a reason.
  3. Print your return label
    Use the prepaid shipping label provided or generate a new one if needed.
  4. Package your items
    Securely package your items with all tags attached in the original or similar packaging.
  5. Ship your return
    Drop off your package at the specified carrier location. We recommend keeping your receipt until the return is processed.

Once we receive your return (typically 5-7 business days after shipping), we'll inspect the items and process your refund within 2-3 business days.

Refund Process

Refunds will be issued to the original payment method used for the purchase. Please note:

  • Credit card refunds may take 5-10 business days to appear on your statement, depending on your financial institution
  • Return shipping costs will be deducted from your refund unless the return is due to a defect, damage, or our error
  • Original shipping charges are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error

Exchanges

We currently don't offer direct exchanges. For a different size or color, please return the original item and place a new order for the desired product. This ensures you get the item you want without delays.

Damaged or Defective Items

If you receive a damaged or defective item, please contact our customer service team within 48 hours of delivery. Provide your order number and clear photos of the damage or defect. We'll work quickly to make it right.

Need Help?

If you have any questions about returns or exchanges, please contact our customer service team for assistance.
